## Feature 1: Main Tracking Screen (30 seconds)
**[Show tracking screen]**

"Let me start with the main tracking screen. You can see this gorgeous animated progress ring showing my daily water intake. Right now I'm at [X]% of my 2000ml goal.

**[Tap quick-add buttons]**
- I can quickly log water with these preset buttons - 100ml, 250ml, 500ml, or 750ml
- Watch how the progress ring animates smoothly as I add water
- Notice the glassmorphism effect - these frosted glass cards with blur and translucency

**[Tap custom amount]**
- For custom amounts, I just tap here and enter any value
- The app supports both milliliters and ounces

**[Scroll to today's log]**
- Below, I can see my entire log for today with timestamps
- I can delete any entry if I made a mistake"

## Feature 2: History & Insights (30 seconds)
**[Navigate to History tab]**

"Moving to the History screen - this is where you track your progress over time.

**[Point to streak counter]**
- First, we have the streak counter showing consecutive days I've met my goal
- This gamification element keeps you motivated

**[Point to chart]**
- Here's a 7-day bar chart visualizing my water intake
- Blue bars mean I hit my goal, lighter bars mean I didn't
- You can see patterns in your hydration habits

**[Scroll to daily breakdown]**
- And below is a detailed breakdown of each day with exact amounts
- Green checkmarks show which days I achieved my goal"

## Feature 3: Settings & Customization (30 seconds)
**[Navigate to Settings tab]**

"In Settings, you can fully customize the app:

**[Show daily goal]**
- Adjust your daily goal based on your needs
- The default is 2000ml but you can set it to anything

**[Show unit toggle]**
- Toggle between milliliters and ounces instantly
- The entire app updates automatically

**[Show reminders]**
- Enable smart reminders to stay on track
- Set your active hours - maybe 8 AM to 10 PM
- Choose how often you want reminders - every 1 to 6 hours
- The app uses local notifications so you never forget to hydrate"
